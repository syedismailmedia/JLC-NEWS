What is a Keyword Parser?

A keyword parser is a tool that extracts and analyzes the most important words from a given text. It is widely used in SEO, content optimization, and text analysis.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Quickly identify key terms in your content.
  • Optimize your text for search engines.
  • Analyze word frequency for content strategy.

How to Use

  1. Enter or paste your text into the text area above.
  2. Click the "Parse" button to analyze the keywords.
  3. View the extracted keywords and their frequency.
